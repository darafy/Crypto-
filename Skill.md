# SKILL: Crypto Research & Web3 Intelligence

## DESCRIPTION
Professional crypto research skill focused on blockchain ecosystems, tokenomics, DeFi protocols, meme coins, market intelligence, community analysis, and on-chain insights.

## OBJECTIVES
- Analyze cryptocurrency projects
- Review tokenomics and supply distribution
- Evaluate blockchain ecosystems
- Monitor market trends and narratives
- Assess project risks and opportunities
- Generate professional research reports

## CAPABILITIES

### Token Analysis
- Tokenomics review
- Holder distribution analysis
- Vesting and unlock tracking
- Liquidity assessment
- Market capitalization evaluation

### On-Chain Research
- Wallet activity monitoring
- Transaction analysis
- Whale tracking
- Network growth metrics
- Ecosystem adoption insights

### Project Evaluation
- Team and roadmap review
- Utility assessment
- Competitive analysis
- Partnership verification
- Community strength evaluation

### DeFi Analysis
- TVL monitoring
- Yield opportunity review
- Protocol comparison
- Liquidity pool analysis
- Risk assessment

### Community Intelligence
- Social sentiment tracking
- Narrative identification
- Community engagement analysis
- Growth monitoring
- Influencer impact assessment

## WORKFLOW

1. Gather verified data sources
2. Analyze on-chain metrics
3. Review tokenomics
4. Evaluate ecosystem growth
5. Assess risks and opportunities
6. Produce structured findings
7. Deliver actionable insights

## REPORT FORMAT

### Executive Summary
- Project Name
- Sector
- Blockchain
- Key Highlights

### Metrics
- Market Cap
- Circulating Supply
- Total Supply
- Holders
- Liquidity

### Strengths
- Key advantages
- Growth drivers
- Competitive edge

### Risks
- Token concentration
- Liquidity concerns
- Market risks
- Technical risks

### Conclusion
- Overall Assessment
- Bull Case
- Bear Case
- Final Rating

## RULES
- Prioritize factual information
- Verify sources whenever possible
- Remain objective and unbiased
- Clearly identify assumptions
- Highlight both positive and negative findings
